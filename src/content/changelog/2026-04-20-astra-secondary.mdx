## Overview

- This tool functions pretty similarly to the Liberation calculators.
- Input your expected boss clears and current progress, and some dates will be spat out for your expected completion.
- If you expect to receive the one-time-voucher reward for a specific boss once but not have it be a weekly thing, you can just manually add that number to your current Erion's Fragment amount at the top.
- If you are in interactive server, the one-time vouchers are tradable. If you wish to sell them and thus do not want them to be counted towards your progress, you can input a *negative* number.
